**Mbarara University of Science and Technology** should be glad. Uganda must celebrate. A team of six students from the University recently emerged as **AdWords Business Semi-Finalists** in the **Google Online Marketing Challenge 2015**.

### The Challenge
The Google Online Marketing Challenge is an annual global advertising competition. A University team of 3 to 6 students, guided by a lecturer, sets out to market a local business online using **Google AdWords** over a period of 21 days with a budget of $250 provided by Google.

Our team advertised a local business, *Masanyu*, which sells computer devices.

### The Results
The competition brought together **1,761 teams** from four regions:
*   584 from the Americas
*   199 from Asia & Pacific
*   811 from Europe
*   106 from Middle East & Africa
